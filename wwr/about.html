About us — Travel Rafting Co.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring adventure, excitement, and unforgettable river experiences to all who join us.

History

Travel Rafting Co. began in 1988 as a small family venture driven by a passion for rivers and outdoor adventure. With only a couple of rafts and a dedicated team, we started guiding visitors through the region´s scenic and challenging waterways. Over the years, we expanded our routes, improved our equipment, and welcomed thousands of guests seeking excitement and connection with nature. Today, we remain committed to offering safe, memorable rafting experiences while honoring the same spirit of adventure that inspired our beginnings.
